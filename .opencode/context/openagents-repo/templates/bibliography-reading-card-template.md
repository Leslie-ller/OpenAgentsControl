# Bibliography Reading Card Template

**Purpose**: Reusable note format for one fully reviewed paper

---

## Template

```markdown
# Reading Card: {paper title}

paper_id: {citation key}
title: {paper title}
authors: {authors}
year: {year}
source: {venue / journal / conference}

## Research Problem

{What problem does this paper actually solve?}

## Method

{Study design, model, experiment, system, or analytical method}

## Key Findings

- finding 1
- finding 2
- finding 3

## Limitations

- limitation 1
- limitation 2

## Reuse Value

- background
- method
- empirical evidence
- contradiction / boundary case

## Misuse Risks

- risk 1
- risk 2

## Suggested Role

{core support | method support | related work | background}

## Evidence Anchors

- p.{page or location}: {claim summary}
- p.{page or location}: {claim summary}

## Next Action

{Send to literature decision | compare with stronger source | keep in queue | archive}
```

---

## Rules

- Prefer paraphrase plus location markers over long quotations.
- Capture both value and limits.
- Keep the role narrow enough to avoid over-citation later.
